Founding of the Club

On February 15th, 2022, Russia started a war against Ukraine. Seeing the attrocities and reality of war on social media put into perspective the privilege that we have as American citizens. Doing nothing about these issues while also being so forunate as to not have to deal with anything similar, was eye opening. This sparked the founding of the Students for Humanitarian Awareness Club. We were officially named a club in march of 2022.

Our Mission

The mission of this club is to raise awareness for humanitarian issues in all parts of the world. We are also seeking to stop the spread of propaganda used to misinform the public on these issues. Not only will we raise awareness through our independant research and posts, we will also help those in need through donation drives and fundraisers.
