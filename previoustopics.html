Russian War Against Ukraine

On February 24, 2022, Russia began an unprovoked attack on Ukraine. In an atempt to invalidate Ukraines sovereignty, Russian President Vladimir Putin planned to send 300,000 troops to invade Ukraine. Russia has commited many war crimes including: torture, rape , and deliberate attacks on civillians. The threat of nucelar war continues to rise as Russia gets pushed back further out of Ukraine. As of October 25, 2022 the war is still raging on; in total around 9,000 Ukrainian and 45,000 Russian soldiers have lost their lives.



What we did:

-We were able to donate nearly $2000 to Ukrainian refugees

-We spread awareness about the war to the Lane Tech community
